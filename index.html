Some useful infomation about the available updrades are:

Warclaw Mastery 1: nlocks the warclaw collection (after completing that you can use your warclaw)
War Gliding Mastery 1: allowes you to glide in territories your world owns.
Provisions Master 4: gives you autoloot in wvw.
Flame Ram Mastery 2: Flame Blast now applies Structural Vulnerability to gates.
Flame Ram Mastery 3: reduce recharge of flame ram skills. With this unlocked you do maximum dps with a ram.
Catapult Mastery 4: increases catapult damage. With this unlocked you do maximum dps with a catapult.
Warclaw Mastery 5: unlocks the sniff skill.
